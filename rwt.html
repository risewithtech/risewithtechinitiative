Rise with Tech

Empowering girls with digital skills and tech confidence to lead, create, and innovate.

About Us

Rise with Tech is a youth-led initiative that inspires and empowers young girls in underserved communities through digital literacy and hands-on technology training. We aim to foster a culture where girls feel confident exploring tech careers and believe they can shape the digital world.

Our Programs

  • Digital Awareness Workshops
  • School Outreach & Motivational Talks
  • Mentorship Sessions with Women in Tech
  • Community Bootcamps & Tech Skills Training
  • Volunteer and Ambassador Training

Our Goals

In the short term, we aim to reach hundreds of girls in schools through outreach programs. In the long run, we envision scaling across Nigeria with mobile tech labs, training thousands of girls, and creating a vibrant network of empowered female changemakers in technology.
